TOUR OF LIFE

Healing, Rest, Connection and Encouragment



After years of ministry we found ourselves to be really tired, and in need of a break. We reached out to a couple of Christian retreat companies for some refuge and they all said the same thing. "That will be 2000$". We, being small town ministers, didn't have that kind of revenue to spend on something like this. We felt discouraged, then all of the sudden an idea popped into our heads. Tour of Life. A ministry to help small town ministers, the under-appreciated and under-resourced, get a time of rest and reclusion
